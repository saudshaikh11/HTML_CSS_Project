Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a receipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cream

Method

  1. Melt butter
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Add cream and eggs
  4. Fold the eggs from the sides as if you are making an omelette
  5. Remove the eggs from the heat when moist.
