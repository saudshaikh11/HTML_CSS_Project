The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her windown for about an hour. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all the other scientific journals her work had appeared in, was a thumped copy of On The Road. It had been Molly's favourite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt> she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of the people who have nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, inspiring unravelling the secret of the Ilama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had somethigng better to do.
