From Garden to Plate

A Potager is a french term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers(edible and non-edible) and herbs are painted with the vegetables to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their olor and form. Many are trained to grow upward A well designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their funtion of providing for a home in a wide area of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
